"Vanishing"
‍

Objects and spaces have memories. Those memories fall into oblivion. Two diners in Hong Kong and Los Angeles. A and B, a telepathic “phone call” between them. How do we understand a foreign story through spaces and objects? 

A Chinese and American devised play, Vanishing is a multimedia journey inviting the audience to think about the narrative potential of everyday objects around us. What are the collective memories of objects and spaces? How do we hold the memories in their tangible forms?
