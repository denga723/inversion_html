About in-version

in-version ensemble was founded in 2024 by a group of theater makers / visual artists / creative writers. in-version ensemble aims to create ensemble-based, devising theater works on and off stage. Their productions inform a wide spectrum of elements — videos, soundscapes, interactive games, and poetry — and how those narrative elements can be important tools and inspirations in creating theatrical texts. In their 2024 season, in-version ensemble is putting on their first devising work Vanishing in Los Angeles and Edinburgh. The members of in-version ensemble are individually involved in various other theater productions, films, installations, and videos under their own names.
